## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit [trainerhubb.app/register](https://trainerhubb.app/register)
2. Enter your email, password, and business information
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your trainer profile

### Initial Setup

After registration, complete these steps:

1. **Profile Setup**: Add your bio, expertise, and location
2. **Availability**: Set your working hours
3. **Packages**: Create your training packages
4. **Public Page**: Build your public-facing page

## Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:

- **Today's Bookings**: Upcoming sessions for today
- **Client Count**: Total active clients
- **Monthly Revenue**: Revenue for the current month
- **Recent Activity**: Latest bookings, payments, and client interactions

## Clients Management

### Adding a Client

1. Navigate to "Clients"
2. Click "Add Client"
3. Enter client information:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone number
   - Notes (optional)
4. Click "Save"

### Managing Clients

- **View Details**: Click on a client to see their full profile
- **Edit**: Update client information
- **Delete**: Remove a client (this cannot be undone)
- **View History**: See all bookings and payments

### Payment Tracking

For each client, you can:
- View payment history
- Record payments manually
- See outstanding balances
- Filter by payment status

## Bookings & Scheduling

### Setting Availability

1. Go to "Settings" > "Availability"
2. Set your working hours for each day
3. Add breaks or blocked times
4. Save changes

### Creating a Booking

1. Navigate to "Bookings"
2. Click "New Booking"
3. Select:
   - Client
   - Date and time
   - Duration
   - Location
   - Notes
4. Click "Create Booking"

### Managing Bookings

- **Confirm**: Confirm a pending booking
- **Reschedule**: Change date/time
- **Cancel**: Cancel a booking
- **Complete**: Mark as completed after the session

## Packages

### Creating a Package

1. Go to "Packages"
2. Click "Create Package"
3. Enter details:
   - Package name
   - Description
   - Price
   - Number of sessions
   - Validity period
4. Click "Save"

### Package Types

- **Session Packages**: Fixed number of sessions
- **Monthly Memberships**: Recurring subscriptions
- **Program Packages**: Multi-week programs

### Assigning Packages

1. Go to a client's profile
2. Click "Assign Package"
3. Select the package
4. Set start date
5. Click "Assign"

## Page Builder

Create professional landing pages to showcase your services.

### Creating a Page

1. Navigate to "Pages"
2. Click "Create Page" or "Choose Template"
3. Add sections:
   - Hero: Main banner with CTA
   - Services: List your services
   - About: Your story
   - Testimonials: Client reviews
   - Pricing: Package prices
   - Contact: Contact form
   - Gallery: Photos
   - FAQ: Frequently asked questions
   - Booking: Booking widget

### Customizing Sections

- Click any section to edit
- Drag sections to reorder
- Delete unwanted sections
- Add new sections from the sidebar

### Publishing Your Page

1. Click "Settings" to add SEO information
2. Click "Preview" to see how it looks
3. Click "Publish" to make it live
4. Share your page: `{your-slug}.trainerhubb.app/{page-slug}`

## Payments & Revenue

### Recording Payments

1. Go to a client's profile
2. Click "Mark as Paid"
3. Enter:
   - Amount
   - Payment method
   - Date
   - Reference ID (optional)
   - Notes
4. Click "Save"

### Payment Methods Supported

- Cash
- Bank Transfer
- Stripe
- PayPal
- Venmo
- Zelle
- Other

### Revenue Reports

Access reports from the "Reports" page:
- Revenue by period
- Revenue by payment method
- Unpaid clients
- Export to CSV

## Workflows & Automation

Automate your communications with clients.

### Creating a Workflow

1. Navigate to "Workflows"
2. Click "Create Workflow"
3. Choose a trigger:
   - Booking created
   - Booking confirmed
   - Payment received
   - Client created
4. Add actions:
   - Send Email
   - Send SMS
   - Update status
5. Save and activate

### Email Templates

1. Go to "Templates" > "Email"
2. Click "Create Template"
3. Add:
   - Template name
   - Subject line
   - Body (use variables like {{client_name}})
4. Save

### SMS Templates

Similar to email templates, but limited to 160 characters.

## Common Issues

### I can't log in

- Check your email and password
- Click "Forgot Password" to reset
- Contact support if still having issues

### My public page isn't showing

- Make sure the page is published
- Check the URL is correct
- Clear your browser cache

### Client didn't receive email

- Check spam folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Check your email sending limits

### Payment not recorded

- Refresh the page
- Check if it appears in payment history
- Contact support if missing
